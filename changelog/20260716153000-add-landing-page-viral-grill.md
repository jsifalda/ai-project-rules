# Add landing-page-viral-grill skill

- Added a skill that audits a landing page for shareability, not conversion.
- 28-criterion rubric across 7 sections. 18 criteria are page-observable and scored from quoted evidence; 10 are product-level and never scored, deferred to a `grill-me` interview instead.
- Why: an existing conversion-focused audit grades whether a visitor will buy. This grades whether a visitor who will never buy would still pass the page on. Different question, different rubric.
- Why the split: pricing, positioning, and founder-visibility criteria aren't decidable from page markup. Scoring them would mean inventing verdicts, so they became interview questions instead.
- No new dependencies.
